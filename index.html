The Adventures of the Six Richards and Friends

Sam

Sam has an insatiable love for adventure and has a particular interest in off-roading. He likes to push himself and his rig to the limit. He once jumped his SUV eight feet off the ground in Death Valley, deploying his air bags on impact. Sam is the glue of the Six Richards & Friends, as he brings all of us together with new adventure ideas and works tirelessly to make things happen.

Josh

Josh loves the outdoors and is fearless in taking on challenges. Josh's broad expertise in wilderness survival along with his calming demeanor has proved an invaluable asset when we were lost, off-roading Jeeps in the closing hours of the trails in Hollister Hills SVRA. Josh is the community builder of the Six Richards & Friends as his overall concern and care for us extend far beyond any expectations.

DJ

DJ adds color to the Six Richards & Friends with his eye for accents to adventures. DJ's particular sense for minutiae has piqued the group's interest in bow-ties, cigars and rhythmic steps. DJ is the gloss of the Six Richards & Friends as his deep appreciation of the web of relationships is manifest through his role as the most ardent cheerleader and publicist of the group.

Dan

Dan is a craftsman who devotes his seemingly unlimited energy to envision, plan and build essential assets for every adventure. Dan's grand vision and attention to detail were the driving force behind the construction of the working, back lit, 12 foot clocktower for the Back to The Future Party (2012). Dan is the motor of the Six Richards & Friends as his infectious enthusiasm pushes projects forward and brings out the best in us.

Nick

Nick is a steady, high level thinker who ensures all adventures run smoothly. Nick's ability to diffuse conflicts and his commitment to the success of all Six Richards & Friends set the tone for an open and encouraging environment. Nick is the universal asset to the Six Richards & Friends because of his unselfish character and willingess to do any job or make any sacrifice for the betterment of the group.

Paul

Paul brings a level of class and polish otherwise unknown to the Six Richards & Friends. Paul's expansive, yet esoteric insights into all things enrich the experience of all adventures. Paul is the soul of the Six Richards & Friends because he represents not only the gallant nature of the group with his aggressive dirt ride maneuvering, but also our collective sophistication through his ability to deliver eloquent, impromptu monologues with finesse.

Drew

Drew represents all that is good about the Six Richards & Friends. A man's man, Drew led the charge in providing sustenance for us in Death Valley by capturing a rabbit, skinning it, gutting it and roasting it for all us to eat. Drew wowed us all as we watched him fix a completely broken down Land Rover in the desert, allowing us to continue our adventure. Drew is the spirit of the Six Richards & Friends, as his presence lifts all of our hearts and inspires us all to become better men.
